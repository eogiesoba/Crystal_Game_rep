CRYSTAL COLLECTOR


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crytal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random target number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts the game will change the value of each crystal.
